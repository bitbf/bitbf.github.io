About
Me

My name is Your Name, and I am a multidisciplinary artist currently based in [City]. My practice spans painting, photography, and installation — each work an attempt to navigate the tension between memory and materiality.

Growing up between cultures has deeply informed my visual language. I am drawn to liminal spaces — the threshold between belonging and estrangement — and I explore these through layered surfaces, fragmented forms, and the quiet persistence of everyday objects.

Artist Statement

"I make work because I need to understand — not the world in its totality, but the small, stubborn fragments that refuse to be translated into language. Each piece begins as a question I cannot yet articulate."

— Your Name, 2024
